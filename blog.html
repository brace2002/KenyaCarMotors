2020 Mercedes-Benz GLB-Class

As the boxy brother to the brand's fastback GLA-class SUV, the Mercedes-Benz GLB-class brings a style all its own to go with its increased practicality. Like the GLA, the GLB250 is powered by a turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine and is available in either front- or all-wheel drive guise. A third row of seats is optional—a segment exclusive—but don't expect to fit full-size humans back there for any length of time; it's a kid-friendly zone. The GLB benefits from Mercedes-Benz's latest MBUX infotainment system, offers a digital instrument cluster that's integrated with the infotainment screen, and can be optioned with all manner of comfort and convenience items.
