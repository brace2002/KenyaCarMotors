2023 Buick LaCrosse

The freshly updated 2020 Buick LaCrosse got its official reveal in China in March, followed by the debut of the range-topping Avenir trim level several weeks later. Highlights for the refresh include a new exterior design that brings Buick’s latest design language to the 2020 Buick LaCrosse.

Engine

4.6-liter, (5,552cc) 32-valve V8 engine, aluminum-alloy block and heads and dry film-coated pistons

Transmission

7-speed Automatic Transmission with Adaptive Shift Control and manual shift mode with Downshift Rev-Matching

Interior Features

Tri-Zone Automatic Temperature Control System with microfilter Advanced Climate Control System with auto recirculation, Plasmacluster® technology, Grape Polyphenol filter [*] Mild-flow vent located on upper dash
